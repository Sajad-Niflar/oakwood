Oakwood Regional Hospital - Colombo Branch

Founded in 1980, Oakwood Regional Hospital’s Colombo branch has been a beacon of healthcare excellence for over four decades. As the flagship facility, it offers 200 beds, including a dedicated critical care unit, and a wide range of specialized services to meet the diverse medical needs of the community. Our hospital is staffed by some of the country’s top consultants, specialists, and nurses, all focused on delivering the highest standards of medical care with compassion and dedication. Equipped with cutting-edge diagnostic and therapeutic technologies, including advanced imaging and surgical tools, the Colombo branch remains at the forefront of medical innovation. We take pride in offering personalized care in a modern, comfortable setting, ensuring that every patient’s experience is as seamless and stress-free as possible. As the main branch, Colombo serves as the heart of our operations, continuously evolving to provide the best healthcare for all.

Services Provided:

  • Outpatient Services
  • General Surgery
  • Cardiology
  • Orthopedics
  • Neurology
  • Maternity Care
  • Diagnostic Imaging
  • Pharmacy Services

Oakwood Regional Hospital - Kandy Branch

In 1995, Oakwood Regional Hospital expanded its services by opening the Kandy branch, designed to bring high-quality healthcare to the Central Province. This facility houses 150 beds in spacious, modern rooms, offering patients the comfort and care they deserve. The Kandy branch is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology, from advanced diagnostic imaging to the latest in surgical equipment, ensuring that patients receive timely and effective treatment. Our team of highly skilled consultants, physicians, and nursing staff is dedicated to delivering personalized care across various specialties, from cardiology to orthopedics. In addition to routine care, the hospital’s emergency unit is on standby 24/7, equipped to handle critical cases with efficiency and expertise. As part of Oakwood Regional Hospital’s mission to make healthcare accessible to all, the Kandy branch plays a crucial role in extending our reach to patients in the Central region, ensuring that they receive top-notch medical care without having to travel far from home.

Oakwood Regional Hospital - Galle Branch

Oakwood Regional Hospital’s Galle branch, opened in 2005, has quickly become a cornerstone of healthcare in the Southern Province. This modern facility is equipped with 120 beds, including specialized units for surgery and intensive care, ensuring that we can meet a wide range of medical needs, from routine checkups to complex surgeries. Our Galle branch boasts some of the latest medical technologies, including advanced imaging systems and state-of-the-art operating rooms, allowing for precise diagnosis and effective treatment. The hospital is staffed by a dedicated team of doctors, surgeons, and nurses, all of whom are committed to delivering compassionate care that is both patient-centered and results-driven. Our facilities include a fully equipped emergency department, ensuring that patients receive immediate attention during critical situations. The Galle branch also focuses on preventive care and wellness, offering health education programs to promote long-term wellbeing. As part of Oakwood Regional Hospital’s mission, the Galle branch is proud to bring world-class medical care to the local community.
